# WA2 — A+ Standard Comparison Chart copy

Text-only. Amazon's Standard Comparison Chart pulls each book's cover image and title automatically from the ASIN — you just supply the chart heading, the four ASINs, and the row data below.

In KDP, go to the A+ Content editor → Add Module → **Standard Comparison Chart**. The module gives you up to 6 product columns and 10 comparison rows. We're using 4 columns and 5 rows.

## Note on chart framing

This chart is positioned for the WA2 reader who already knows what they're getting from WarMage. WA1 is in column 1 to catch anyone who arrived at Book 2 first (Amazon's search does that more than you'd think). The three UA columns are the catalog-breadth pitch.

If you'd rather drop WA1 and run a tighter three-column "Try the Ravenwick Chronicle next" chart, that's also a fine read — say the word and I'll cut it down.
