## Submission checklist

- [ ] All four images uploaded under 2MB each (they are — biggest is the header at ~130KB)
- [ ] All alt text under 80 characters
- [ ] No external links in any body text
- [ ] No mentions of Kindle Unlimited, "free", "limited time", or pricing
- [ ] No mentions of other retailers
- [ ] ASINs for the four comparison-chart books filled in
- [ ] Preview shows all four modules render top-to-bottom without overlap

Then hit **Submit for approval**. Amazon usually approves within 7 business days. Once approved, the A+ Content appears below the product description on the WA1 detail page.
