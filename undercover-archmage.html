About the Book

On the run from a government hit squad, an ex-black-ops operator takes cover as Professor of Defensive Magics at Ravenwick Academy. The cover story is paper-thin. The problem is bigger than that. He's a Null — magic-immune, off the spectrum, a void in the wards. To the deans, he's invisible. To the most dangerous women on campus, he's the only thing in the building they haven't already cracked.

They have fireballs. He has a Glock and a quarter-century of dirty tradecraft. The food chain just got rewritten and nobody told the food.

For readers who…

love magic-academy fantasy with the safety taken off — students who would cheerfully kill you, a faculty meeting that could escalate to a tactical breach, and a protagonist whose superpower is being the boring one in a room full of weather-summoning grad students.
