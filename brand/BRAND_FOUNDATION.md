# Alex Savage — Brand Foundation

A working brand document for the Alex Savage pen name, published under the **Savagery & Co.** imprint. This file is the source of truth. The GIMP scripts, the website CSS, the cover assets, and the social templates all pull from here.

---

## 1. Who Alex Savage is

**Pen name. Action haremlit author.** Writes male-protagonist haremlit with two registers:

- **Sensual / hardboiled fantasy** — the *Undercover Archmage* / *Ravenwick Chronicle* line. Black-ops operator under deep cover at a magic academy. Mature, intimate, painterly cover art.
- **Slice-of-life LitRPG** — the *WarMage of Arkley* line. Supremacy-grade mage raising his daughter when the apocalypse turns up next door. Lighter, character-forward, illustrated cover art.

Both share the author's voice — overpowered men with cover stories that are coming apart, and the women determined to be in the room when it happens.

**Imprint:** Savagery & Co. (the back-end identity — Patreon, copyright line, eventual merch).

**Core promise to the reader:** competence porn meets character chemistry. The hero is good at what he does; the women see through him anyway; the world keeps making things harder. Books deliver action, romance, and momentum — in that order.

---

## 2. Visual personality

Mature. Painterly. Confident. Not grim, not glossy. The brand reads like a **modern romantasy imprint with an action edge** — purple-and-gold like an old grimoire that's been kept somewhere warm, with a flash of coral when the cover needs to flirt.

**Avoid:** dragon-fantasy clichés, neon LitRPG-screen aesthetics, generic romance pastels, anything that looks AI-generated by default.

**Embrace:** rich saturated colors, classical typography, hand-drawn marks, the feel of something deliberately made.

## 3. Color system

The website palette refined into named roles. Every cover, every social post, every brand surface pulls from this list and only this list.

| Role | Hex | Used for |
|---|---|---|
| Primary deep | `#160C24` | Background base, dark cover bands, deep negative space |
| Primary mid | `#1F1336` | Secondary surfaces, gradient mid-tone |
| Surface 1 | `#271847` | Card surfaces, raised UI panels |
| Surface 2 | `#321F58` | Hover/active states |
| **Brand gold** | `#E8B756` | Primary accent — author name underline, imprint mark, illuminated detail |
| Gold soft | `#F4D27A` | Highlights, hover states on gold elements |
| **Brand coral** | `#FF7E94` | Romance signal — quote card accents, kicker text, attention call-outs |
| Coral soft | `#FFB1BD` | Coral hover/secondary use |
| **Brand cream** | `#F5EFE6` | Light surfaces, parchment, author-name face on dark covers |
| Muted lavender | `#B1A2CC` | Body text on dark surfaces, secondary copy |

**New addition — Cover Band tone:**

| Role | Hex | Used for |
|---|---|---|
| Cover band | `#1A0F2C` at 88% opacity over artwork | A fixed dark surface at top or bottom of every cover where the **author name lockup** sits. Keeps Alex Savage readable on any artwork. |

## 4. Typography

| Face | Role | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **Fraunces** | Display + author lockup | Author name on covers, hero headings on website, social card headlines |
| **Cinzel** | Imprint face | "Savagery & Co." wordmark only. Reserved. Don't use elsewhere. |
| **Inter** | Body | All body text — website, ebook description blurbs, social card subhead copy |
| **Caveat** | Accent only | Sparingly — kicker text, handwritten quote attribution, signature flourish |

**Author lockup spec:**
- Face: Fraunces, weight 600 (Semibold)
- Tracking: +0.04em
- Case: Mixed case ("Alex Savage" not "ALEX SAVAGE") — reads more author, less product
- Color: `#F5EFE6` (brand cream) on dark band, OR `#160C24` (primary deep) on light surface
- Position: Bottom band of cover, centered, with a thin gold underline rule (1px, 60% width)

## 5. Brand marks

### Three distinct marks, three distinct jobs:

**a) Alex Savage Wordmark** — the everyday brand layer
- Just the text "Alex Savage" in the lockup spec above
- Lives on every cover, social card, ad, signature
- Where the painterly portrait won't fit, the wordmark goes

**b) Alex Savage Personal Mark (hooded figure)** — to be reworked
- Painterly hooded illustration, used as hero/about/press kit only
- Current version needs refinement (see brief in `HOODED_FIGURE_BRIEF.md`)
- Has a simplified SVG silhouette version for favicon/avatar/watermark use

**c) Savagery & Co. Imprint Mark** — the publisher's stamp
- Cinzel "SAVAGERY & CO." with arrow-pierced-book glyph
- Currently exists in two variants (primary on light, light on dark)
- Lives on: spine, back cover, Patreon page, eventual merch tag
- Does **not** go on the front cover front-and-center — it's a subtle signature

## 6. Series identity layer

Each series gets a **color anchor** drawn from the brand palette. Used as the spine color, the social card accent strip, and the back-cover color block. Allows readers to clock the series at a glance without changing cover art style.

| Series | Color anchor | Hex | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
| The Ravenwick Chronicle (UA) | Brand gold | `#E8B756` | Aligns with academic/magical illumination, gold-on-purple matches the UA cover style |
| The WarMage of Arkley (WA) | Brand coral | `#FF7E94` | Warmer, slice-of-life signal, plays well with the lighter WA art direction |

If/when a third series launches, third color is reserved from: muted lavender `#B1A2CC` or a new tone added here.

## 7. Usage rules

**On book covers:**
- Author lockup always present at top OR bottom, on Cover Band tone
- Title typography is series-specific (UA uses one style, WA uses another) — that's intentional genre signaling
- Savagery & Co. imprint mark on spine and back cover, never larger than 8% of cover height
- Series color anchor appears as a thin (4–6px) rule above or below the author lockup

**On social cards (1080×1080 / 1080×1920):**
- Author lockup top, Savagery & Co. mark bottom-right small
- Background: brand palette gradient or solid `#160C24`
- Gold for emphasis, coral for romance/CTA, never both at high intensity in the same composition

**On the website:**
- Already largely locked. Future changes pull from this doc, not from designer intuition.

**Avoid:**
- The hooded figure illustration on covers (too busy, fights the artwork)
- Cinzel anywhere except the imprint mark
- Coral and gold both at 100% saturation in the same composition
- Any color outside this palette
