Long — 200 words

Alex Savage is a pen-name author writing action haremlit — the disciplined, character-forward end of the genre. Two ongoing series, six books in print, and a Patreon community (Savagery & Co.) where early chapters drop every Friday.

The Ravenwick Chronicle is a magic-academy fantasy with the safety off. An ex-black-ops operator takes cover as Professor of Defensive Magics at Ravenwick Academy, an elite postgrad institution where the students are dangerous and the wards are alive. He's a Null — magic-immune, off the spectrum, invisible to the wards and a beacon to the most dangerous women on campus. They have fireballs. He has a Glock. The cover story is failing. Four books, ongoing.

The WarMage of Arkley is its slice-of-life mirror: a Supremacy-grade mage settles in a coastal town to raise his daughter; the backyard sprouts a dimensional tear; the System interface starts nagging; the neighbours take a pointed interest. Two books, ongoing. Cozy LitRPG with marshmallows and Abyssal monsters in roughly equal measure.
