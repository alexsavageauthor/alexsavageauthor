About Alex

Alex Savage is a pen name. The books are real. The rest is none of your business.

I write action haremlit — overpowered men trying very hard not to be noticed, in worlds that won’t stop noticing them. Expect tradecraft, magic-on-magic violence, single-dad domesticity ruined by inter-dimensional tears, fireballs answered with 9mm, and women who refuse to let any of it stay quiet.

Two ongoing series, more on the way. If you like your heroes hard-edged, your magic systems with stakes, and your fight scenes with both punchlines and ammunition counts — you’re in the right place.
